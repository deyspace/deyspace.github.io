a1: modeling and laser cutting

+ Soure file: Deyi's Cuttle PressKit
+ laser cutter settings: 10S 50P 60F - SUCCESS!
+ write up of process below :)



Press kit requirements:

1/ at least 30 instances of at least one unique part
2/ the parts must have slots that press fit into the other
3/ parts in the kit and be laser cut out of cardboard
4/ fit should be such that the parts don't come apart with a light shaking


I wanted to make something that my daughter could play with, maybe something architectural? making buildings...?


Inspo from: Happy architect toy on Amazon.com

I wanted to make something that my daughter could play with, maybe something architectural? making buildings...? But it soon felt a little boring after I tried to 'build a village', so I shifted gear and thought about what can I make with 30 pieces that are similar..?



Prototyping

 Tested out an idea quickly with thin carboard. I initially made 90 degree cuts on the cirlces, but wanted more space for the 30 pieces that will fit around the edge of the circle, so I angled the cut.





I first started trying to use Rhino...but it broke my brain, so I went back to Cuttle, it's UI was cuttlier than Rhino:)
