Initial concept exploration
I want to fabricate an interactive object that acts like a checklist and helps users complete tasks

Intended used case/context:

+ my partner has ADHD and needs help making sure he's packed everything before he goes to pay basketball (70% of the the time, he forgets one of the following: shoes, basketball, extra shirts, socks, water). He also doesn't always remember to use a digital checklist, espeically if he is leaving in a hurry, so a physical checklist in context (attached to his bad) would be more effective at reminding him what he needs to bring

+ my daughter is having trouble going throuhg her bedtime routine and I want to make an interactive checklist thing to help her get to bed quickly while doing all the things she needs to do
